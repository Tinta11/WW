Post Break-up Wines (by Whines).

This is your comprehensive guide to the kinds of wines you should drink depending on the type of break-up you're dealing with.
If it was mutual… time to CELEBRATE!! Welcome your bestie back into the fabulous single life with a nice bottle of bubbly. Of course corner-store prosecco could do, but why settle there when you could go for something a tad more fun? We suggest a nice Franciacorta, an Italian blend from the north that is fruity and fresh. It’s sad of course, but this wine can help you look forward to all the fun times to come (and already making memories with your best girls).
If it was for the best but not mutual… this situation just sucks. Drink responsibly. But a glass or two of a nice, sweet and sipp-able Merlot is perfect. Plus after a glass or two of red you’ll be too happy about your friendship and 'how beautiful everyone looks tonight!’ to really care anyway.
If he’s just an all-around jerk… this calls for a nice drink-able red. It’s finally your chance to tell your friend all the things you think about her former sig other, e.g. “Whats with his hair”, “What was his problem” and “Is he a kleptomanic”. (May or may not be pulled from Kels and Ce experience). We suggest your go for a solid, likeable Pinot Noir.
