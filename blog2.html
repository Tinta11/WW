Best Wine Bars for a Girl's Night: London Edition

There are great wine bars for girls nights out all over the world. We have and will continue to review all of these - from Berlin to NYC.
For this blog, however, we focus on a city particularly close to our heart - London. Here, in no particular order, are the best places to take your girlfrieds - vetted by your own favorite casual connoisseurs.


Compagnie des Vins Surnaturels Seven Dials - Neil's Yard
Compagnie des Vins is one of the cutest places in downtown London. You can cozy into the mismatched furniture with a wide variety of different wines - although we recommending asking the local staff for their recommendations. The best part of this place is the close proximity to Neils Yard and downtown London, as a central meeting place where the whole crew can get together.
Visit their website.


Sager & Wilde - Hackney
Sager & Wilde is adorable. It's a mix between a restaurant and a wine bar - meaning that you can quite literally get grilled cheese with your glass of Chardonnay. If this doesn't say place to get everything off your chest, we don't know what does. From venting about bad days at work to recounting exciting first dates, this is the place to meet up.
Visit their website.


28°-50° Wine Workshop & Kitchen - Marylebone
For those adventurous friends who want to actually try a bunch of wines and talk about them, instead of just using the wine as an excuse to catch up about life, this is the place to go. You can try pouring menus of 15 red and 15 white wines, all with sommelier guidance. Truly a must-visit for all our fellow wine-lovers.
Visit their website.
